Rougail

Rougail Saucisse is a flavorful and hearty dish from Réunion Island, known for its bold combination of spicy and savory flavors. It features sausage (typically smoked or fresh) cooked in a rich, aromatic tomato sauce, seasoned with a variety of local spices like garlic, ginger, and turmeric, often with a touch of chili for heat.
The dish is commonly served with rice, making it a perfect comfort food enjoyed by locals and visitors alike. Rougail Saucisse is a true reflection of the island’s multicultural heritage, blending French, Indian, and African culinary influences into a deliciously unique creation.
